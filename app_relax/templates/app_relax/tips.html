Anxiety tips

Did you know...

  • Regular exercise helps control anxiety
  • Sleeping hours are very important, 6 to 8 hours of sleep is necessary.
  • Avoid high consumption of alcoholic beverages
  • Avoid all drug use, avoid caffeine and exciting substances such as energy drinks.

Relaxing techniques

  • Distraction is a technique, try talking to someone, singing your favorite song or doing an activity that you enjoy.
  • Slow diaphragmatic breathing: It consists of placing one hand on the chest and the other on the stomach, inhale until you feel the lungs fill with air, and exhale the air without the hand on the chest moving. Practice this several times a day Do not use this method if you do not feel 100% comfortable.
  • Self-instructions: Try to make cards that help you, memorize them and repeat them every time you face situations or sensations. Always carry them with you. Adapt and modify them according to your needs.
  • Positive imagination is your help: Change your thoughts, a tip is to imagine yourself in a situation facing what happened and achieving it, visualize your feelings when you achieve it. visualize your feelings when you achieve it, practice it several times and identify your thoughts and what they cause. Stay positive :)
  • Do not be afraid to face, feelings and anxiety diminish if you stop trying to run away from them and accept them.
  • Practice relaxation techniques daily, they change depending on the person so look for the one that makes you feel more comfortable.
